One Signal. Infinite Threats.

Last Signal is a fast-paced survival action game where you are humanity's final broadcast — a lone beacon pulsing against an ever-expanding void. Upgrade your signal, unlock devastating weapons, and push deeper into uncharted frequencies.

Wave Survival

Face endless waves of hostile interference patterns. Each wave brings new enemy types, formations, and escalating danger.

Deep Upgrades

Choose from dozens of signal amplifiers, frequency modulators, and defensive protocols. Every run creates a unique build.

Weapon Arsenal

Unlock 15+ weapons — from pulse cannons to orbital beams. Each weapon evolves with synergies and power-ups.

Equipment System

Find and equip relics that fundamentally change your playstyle. Stack passive effects for devastating combos.

Roguelite Runs

Permanent progression between runs. Unlock new signals, game modes, and story fragments as you push further.

Lore Fragments

Piece together the mystery of what happened to everyone else. Every signal you find carries a fragment of the truth.
