Privacy Policy

Your data, your rules — Builder of Ages has your back

Updated March 2026
🚀
01
Introduction

Welcome, survivor. We built Last Signal to be fun — not to snoop on you. This policy explains what data we collect, why we need it, who we share it with, and how you can take control. Transparency isn't optional for us.

📋
02
What We Collect

We collect the minimum data needed to run and improve the game:

  • Device info — Device model, OS version, unique identifiers
  • Gameplay analytics — Progress, levels completed, time played, in-game behavior
  • Purchase history — Items bought, currency amounts, transactions
  • Ad interaction — Impressions, clicks, engagement (with your consent)
  • Account data — Player name, profile info, settings
  • Crash reports — Logs, performance metrics, error data

04
Data Retention
  • Active accounts: Data retained while your account is active
  • After deletion: Personal data purged within 30 days
  • Analytics: Anonymized data may be kept up to 2 years
  • Ad networks: Retain data per their own policies
🛡️
05
Children's Privacy

Last Signal is not intended for children under 13. We use an age verification gate on first launch. We do not knowingly collect personal info from children under 13. If we discover we have, we'll delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe a child under 13 has created an account should contact us at lastsignalgames@gmail.com with proof of identity.

07
Ad Tracking & Consent

We use ad networks that may track activity for personalized ads. You're in control:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads
  • In-game: We collect consent through our in-game dialog and respect your choices

Security

We use industry-standard security — encryption in transit and at rest. No system is perfect, but we take it seriously. If you think your data has been compromised, contact us immediately at lastsignalgames@gmail.com.
