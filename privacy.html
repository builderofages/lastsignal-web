Last Signal - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

p>

Introduction

Last Signal ("the Game") is developed and published by the Game Developer ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play Last Signal on iOS, Android, or PC.

Information We Collect

Gameplay Data: We collect anonymous gameplay data including levels completed, heroes used, weapons equipped, and session duration. This data helps us improve game balance and player experience.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and a unique device identifier for analytics and crash reporting purposes.

Purchase Information:strong> In-app purchases are processed through Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or the relevant platform. We do not directly collect or store your payment information. Purchase records are managed through RevenueCat.

Advertising Data: Our advertising partner AppLovin MAX may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to operate and improve the Game, process in-app purchases and verify entitlements, serve ads (for non-premium users), analyze gameplay patterns for balancing, and provide customer support.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat: For managing in-app purchases and subscriptions.

AppLovin MAX:strong> For serving advertisements to non-premium users.

Unity Analytics: For anonymous gameplay analytics.

Children's Privacy

Last Signal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Anonymous gameplay analytics are retained for up to 24 months. Purchase records are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access your personal data, request deletion of your data, opt out of personalized advertising, and request a copy of your data in a portable format.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
