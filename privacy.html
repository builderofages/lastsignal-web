1. Introduction

Welcome to Last Signal, developed by Builder of Ages. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your data. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the Last Signal mobile game and related services.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the game experience:

  • Device Information: Device ID, device model, operating system, and unique identifiers
  • Gameplay Analytics: Game progress, levels completed, achievements earned, time played, and in-game behavior
  • In-App Purchase History: Records of items purchased, currencies acquired, and transaction amounts
  • Ad Interaction Data: Ad impressions, clicks, and engagement metrics (collected with your consent)
  • Account Data: Player name, profile information, and game settings
  • Technical Data: Crash logs, performance metrics, and error reports

3. Third-Party SDKs and Data Sharing

We integrate with third-party partners to enhance gameplay and monetization. These partners process data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

AppLovin (Advertising)

AppLovin provides advertisement serving and analytics. AppLovin may collect and process device identifiers, IP addresses, and advertising data to deliver personalized ads. AppLovin Privacy Policy

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

RevenueCat manages our in-app purchase system. RevenueCat receives transaction data including purchase receipts, app user IDs, and subscription information. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Supabase (Cloud Saves & Backend)

Supabase provides backend services for cloud saves and leaderboard data. Your device ID, player statistics, and game progress may be stored on Supabase servers. Supabase Privacy Policy

Firebase (Analytics)

Firebase collects anonymized gameplay analytics, crash reports, and performance data to help us understand game usage and stability. Firebase Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Gameplay data is retained while your account is active
  • After Account Deletion: We delete personal data within 30 days of account deletion requests, except where required by law
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized analytics may be retained for up to 2 years for improvement purposes
  • Ad Data: Ad networks may retain data according to their own retention policies

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Last Signal is not intended for children under 13.

The game implements an age verification gate. Users must be 13 or older to create an account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Parents or guardians who believe a child under 13 has created an account should contact us at lastsignalgames@gmail.com with proof of identity.

6. Your Privacy Rights

GDPR Rights (European Union)

If you are located in the EU, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Right to Objection: Object to certain data processing
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data

CCPA Rights (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to Know: Request what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information collected from you
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: No discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at lastsignalgames@gmail.com with your device ID or account identifier, a clear description of your request, and proof of identity if required by law. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

7. Ad Tracking and Consent

We use third-party advertising networks that may track your activity to serve personalized ads. You can manage ad preferences through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable ad tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Manage Your Account > Data & Privacy > Ad settings

We also collect ad consent through in-game prompts and honor your choices.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at lastsignalgames@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of Last Signal indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
