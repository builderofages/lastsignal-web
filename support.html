Common Transmissions

When does Last Signal launch?

+
We're currently in active development. Sign up on our homepage to be the first to know when we enter beta and when the full launch drops. We'll also announce on our social channels.

What platforms will the game be on?

+
Last Signal is being built for iOS and Android first. We're designing for mobile-first gameplay with touch controls optimized for fast-paced wave survival. Other platforms may follow based on community demand.

Is the game really free to play?

+
Yes. The full game is free to download and play with no content paywalls. We offer optional cosmetic purchases and convenience items, but nothing that affects gameplay balance. You can experience every weapon, upgrade, and story fragment without spending a cent.

How do I report a bug?

+
Send us an email at the address below with "BUG REPORT" in the subject line. Include your device model, OS version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings are incredibly helpful. We read every report.

Can I request a feature?

+
Absolutely. Drop us an email with "FEATURE REQUEST" in the subject. We genuinely track every suggestion. Some of our best ideas have come from early community feedback. Your signal matters.

My progress was lost. Can it be recovered?

+
If you were signed in with a cloud save account, your progress should sync automatically. If you're experiencing sync issues, make sure you have a stable connection and try restarting the app. For persistent issues, contact us with your player ID and we'll investigate.

How does the upgrade system work?

+
Each run, you earn signal fragments and experience. Between runs, you can spend these on permanent upgrades that carry forward. During runs, you choose from randomized power-ups at each level. The combination of permanent progression and per-run choices means every session feels different.
